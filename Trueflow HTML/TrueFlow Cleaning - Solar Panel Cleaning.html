Our 6-Step Solar Cleaning Process

Safe, effective, and scientifically proven methods

1

System Assessment

We inspect your solar array, note panel condition, and identify problem areas like bird droppings or mineral deposits.

2

Dry Debris Removal

Soft brushing removes loose dirt, leaves, and debris without scratching the panel surface.

3

Pure Water Wash

Deionized water cleaning dissolves mineral deposits and grime without leaving residue or streaks.

4

Frame & Mount Cleaning

We clean panel frames, mounting hardware, and ensure proper drainage to prevent future buildup.

5

Final Inspection

Post-cleaning inspection ensures all panels are spotless and system is ready for optimal performance.

6

Performance Report

Optional before/after power output readings show the immediate efficiency improvement.
